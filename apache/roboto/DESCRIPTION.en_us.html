Roboto sans serif has an impeccable uniformity with a good dose of easiness. It’s a dual-natured type of the sans serif family that is mechanical, and at the same time features friendly and open curves. The font doesn’t distort its form to force a rigid rhythm, instead the letters settle into their natural width. This aids in legibility and gives it a harmonious rhythm that works well for both text and headlines

Updated July 2020: Upgraded to a variable font with Weight and Width axes, that closely match the previous static fonts released as two families, this one and a sibling Roboto Condensed family.

Roboto is part of a superfamily set that includes Roboto Slab and Roboto Mono

To report issues or contribute, see github.com/TypeNetwork/Roboto. This repository also contains further font builds for different platforms.
