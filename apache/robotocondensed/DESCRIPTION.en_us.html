Roboto Condensed mimics the same impeccable uniformity as the rest of the Roboto family, but it differs by exhibiting a technical refinement. Be it poster design or editorial layout, the Roboto Condensed sans serif typeface is a worthy addition to your toolbox.

Roboto Condensed can be used alongside the normal Roboto family and the Roboto Slab family.

To contribute, see github.com/google/roboto
