  <h2>About Batista</h2>

  <p>

    Batista is a versatile typeface family, currently including Regular, Italic, and Bold styles. 

    It is designed for both digital and print applications, featuring clean lines, open shapes, 

    and a balanced structure. This makes Batista highly readable at small sizes while retaining 

    elegance at larger display sizes.

  </p>


  <h2>Features</h2>

  <ul>

    <li>Supports Latin alphabet with uppercase and lowercase letters.</li>

    <li>Includes numerals, punctuation, and basic symbols.</li>

    <li>Available in multiple styles: Regular, Italic, and Bold.</li>

    <li>Designed for high legibility in body text and headings.</li>

  </ul>


  <h2>Usage</h2>

  <p>

    Batista can be used freely under the Apache License 2.0. It is suitable for web, print, 

    and user interface design projects.

  </p>
