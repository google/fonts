Based in Pati, Central Java, alphArtype is a personal typefoundry that Agung Rohmat created when he started making fonts in 2018. He is a freelancer designer and got interested in making fonts with a handwritten style. His most popular fonts are "Holiday" and "Collection" and their users come from various countries in the world and from several large companies such as Vevo, Boots and Unilever.
