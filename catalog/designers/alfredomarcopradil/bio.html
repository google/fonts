Alfredo Marco Pradil is a type designer and founder of Hanken Design Co.® foundry. He graduated with a Fine Arts degree at Batangas State University and has been designing typefaces since 2012. His custom typeface work includes the Alienware® typeface for Dell®, Extremis Compakt for furniture design company Extremis®, and Ampersans for Ace & Tate.
