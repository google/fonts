Andres Torresi graduated from Universidad Nacional de Cuyo (Mendoza), with a Specialization in Typeface Design from Universidad de Buenos Aires. Working as a type designer and font developer since 2009, collaborating with clients, foundries and publications including Google, ReType, Omnibus Type, Hype for Type and Katachi Magazine. Taught Typeface Design at CDT-UBA (Universidad de Buenos Aires) in 2016, and has given guest lectures there and at Universidad Nacional de Cuyo, as well as private type design workshops. Member of the Dalton Maag team from 2016 to 2025, contributing to dozens of projects as Project Lead, Font Developer and team manager. Currently Director of HT Fonts, an award-winning London-based font foundry.
