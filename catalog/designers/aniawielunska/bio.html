Ania Wieluńska is a graphic and type designer. She is a PhD student and head of the typedesign studio at the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw. She is a laureate of the TDC Beatrice Warde scholarship, funded by Monotype (NYC). She is the author of "Lazarus" and co-author of "Bona Nova" and "Półtawski Nowy" typefaces.
