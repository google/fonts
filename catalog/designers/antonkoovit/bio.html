Anton Koovit is an Estonian type designer based in Tallinn, and the founder of the type foundry Tipo (formerly known as exljbris Font Foundry). He studied at the renowned Type and Media program at KABK (Royal Academy of Art) in The Hague, Netherlands.

Koovit is known for designing highly successful typeface families including Museo, Museo Sans, Calluna, and Calluna Sans. His work is characterized by a balance between functionality and distinctive personality, making his fonts popular choices for both text and display purposes.
