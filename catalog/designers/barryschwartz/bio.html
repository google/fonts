Barry Schwartz is an American type designer and the founder of The League of Moveable Type, one of the first open-source type foundries dedicated to creating high-quality free fonts. He has been a significant contributor to the open-source typography movement since the late 2000s.

Schwartz specializes in reviving classic typefaces, meticulously recreating historical designs for modern digital use. His work includes revivals of designs by Frederic Goudy, Rudolf Ruzicka, and other masters of early 20th century typography.
