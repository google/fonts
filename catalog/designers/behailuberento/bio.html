Behailu Berento is a typegace and graphic designer based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. With years of experience in the type industry, he is the founder of TypeHabesha, a digital foundry specializing in multi-script branding. Behailu’s work focuses intensely on the intersection of Ethiopic and Latin writing systems, exploring how modern design principles can support traditional scripts without sacrificing cultural heritage. He develops highly functional, open-source typefaces that bring authentic Ethiopian typography to the global stage.
