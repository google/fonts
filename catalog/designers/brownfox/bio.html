Brownfox is a type design studio based in Bangkok, Thailand, specializing in creating typefaces with Thai and Latin script support. The foundry focuses on developing high-quality fonts that serve the needs of both Thai and international typography.

Their work is characterized by modern, geometric designs that maintain excellent readability across both Thai and Latin characters. Multiple Brownfox typefaces are available on Google Fonts, making Thai typography more accessible to designers and developers worldwide.
