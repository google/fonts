Carrois and Edenspiekermann was a collaboration between two German design studios that worked together on typeface design projects from 2012 to 2015. Carrois, founded by Ralph du Carrois (now bBox Type GmbH), is a type design studio specializing in corporate typography. Edenspiekermann AG was an international design agency founded by legendary typographer Erik Spiekermann.

The collaboration is best known for creating Fira Sans, the typeface family commissioned by Mozilla for Firefox OS. The partnership combined Carrois's technical expertise in type design with Edenspiekermann's brand design capabilities.
