Constanza Artigas Preller is a typeface and graphic designer from South America. Her work is characterized by cultural and historical inspirations from Latin American heritage.

She designed Inika in 2011, a serif typeface inspired by Easter Island's Rapa Nui language and culture. The name means "ink" in Rapa Nui. She also co-designed Homenaje with Agustina Mingote, a narrow grotesque sans-serif inspired by bronze cemetery lettering found in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
