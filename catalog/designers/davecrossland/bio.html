Dave Crossland is a type designer and open-source fonts advocate who works at Google Fonts. He is based in Denver, Colorado. Crossland completed his MA in Typeface Design (MATD) at the University of Reading in 2008, where his dissertation focused on "The Free Font Movement."

He is the original designer of Cantarell, a humanist sans-serif typeface that was added to Google Fonts in 2010 and became the default user interface font for GNOME. At Google, he has been a major contributor to the Google Fonts project with over 890 contributions to the repository.
