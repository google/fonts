Denis Jacquerye is a font and language engineer based in Luxembourg. Born in Lubumbashi, Zaire (now the Democratic Republic of the Congo), he holds a BSc in Computer Science with a minor in Linguistics from McGill University in Montreal. He specializes in extending open-source fonts to support African orthographies in the Latin script and is a co-leader of the DejaVu font project. He designed the Molengo typeface, available on Google Fonts. In 2020, Google Fonts commissioned him as a language consultant to expand the Questrial font to include an African Latin glyph set.
