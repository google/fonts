Fontamin is an independent digital type foundry founded in March 2020 by Amin Abedi. Based in Iran, it focuses on designing and distributing high-quality Persian fonts. The platform offers a growing collection of original typefaces, combining contemporary aesthetics with careful attention to readability and functionality. Alongside its commercial library, Fontamin also shares insights and resources through its blog, contributing to the development of Persian typography and design culture.
