Frank Grießhammer is a German type designer who worked at Adobe as a senior type designer. He is known for his work on the Source Han Sans and Source Han Serif pan-CJK typeface families, massive collaborative projects supporting Chinese, Japanese, and Korean scripts.

Beyond his type design work, Grießhammer has contributed significantly to font tooling and the open-source font development ecosystem. He has been involved in developing tools for font production and has shared his expertise through talks and workshops at typography conferences.
