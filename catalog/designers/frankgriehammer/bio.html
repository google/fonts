Frank Grießhammer is a type designer and font developer at Adobe. He studied graphic design at HBKsaar in Saarbrücken and ISIA in Florence, and graduated with a Master of Design in Type and Media from KABK in The Hague in 2010. He joined Adobe in 2011, where he designed Source Serif, the serif companion to Source Sans. The Latin, Greek, and Cyrillic glyphs in Source Han Serif are also his work. His other typefaces include HWT Tuscan Extended, Stempel Elan, and Chidori. Source Serif 4 is available on Google Fonts.
