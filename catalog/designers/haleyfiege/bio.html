Haley Fiege is a Canadian type designer and illustrator based in Montreal. She creates playful, expressive typefaces that often blend illustration with typography, bringing a unique artistic sensibility to font design.

Fiege is best known for her collaboration with artist Shantell Martin on Shantell Sans, a handwriting-inspired variable font released on Google Fonts in 2023. The typeface captures Martin's distinctive drawing style in a functional font family with multiple weights and an italic axis.
