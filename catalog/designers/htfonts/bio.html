HT Fonts is a London based type foundry with a deep respect for design and typography. Founded in 2009 in Argentina, the company began as a place to meet, cooperate, and share experiences while collaborating on academic and commercial projects. HT Fonts develops custom and retail fonts with libre, proprietary, or exclusive licenses, and is strongly committed to creating innovative and functional type. Their award-winning work has been recognized by Letter.2, Tipos Latinos, and the Bienal Iberoamericana de Diseño.
