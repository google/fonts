Jasper de Waard is a self-taught type designer from Rotterdam, the Netherlands, who runs Bureau Roffa, a boutique type foundry. His fascination with typography began early - he published his first typeface on MyFonts at the age of twelve. After an internship at Dalton Maag, he founded Bureau Roffa.

Beyond type design, Jasper pursues a parallel career in cognitive science. He is currently a PhD student at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam in the Department of Cognitive Psychology, conducting experimental research on visual attention.
