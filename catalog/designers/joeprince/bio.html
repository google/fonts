Joe Prince is a type designer based in Los Angeles, California. He has designed nine font families available on Google Fonts, including Maven Pro, Varela Round, Questrial, Handlee, Squada One, Varela, Geostar, and Geostar Fill. NTR, also on Google Fonts, was a collaboration with Appaji Ambarisha Darbha and Purushoth Kumar Guthula.
