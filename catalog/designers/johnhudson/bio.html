John spends a small amount of his time designing typefaces, and the rest of his time making them. He is co-founder of Tiro Typeworks, a small foundry specializing in custom font solutions for multilingual publishing and computing. John has created or collaborated on typefaces for Arabic, Bengali, Burmese, Cyrillic, Devanagari, Ethiopic, Greek, Gurmukhi, Hebrew, IPA, Javanese, Kannada, Latin, Odia, Sinhalese, Soyombo, Telugu, Thai, and other writing systems. He is an expert contributor to Unicode, and a member of the W3C Web Fonts Working Group.
