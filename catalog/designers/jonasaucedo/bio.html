Graphic and type designer, founder of the Mexican type foundry Non Foundry. Throughout his professional career, some of his projects have been featured in various books and exhibitions, as well as recognized with awards in the design field.

Some of his most recent collaborations include Typodarium 2025 (Verlag Hermann Schmidt), Yearbook of Type No. 6 (Slanted Publishers), Curious Typeface No. 2 (Offcuts Publisher), and Typeone Mag No. 10 (Type01).
