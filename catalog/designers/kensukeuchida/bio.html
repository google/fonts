Kensuke Uchida is a Japan-based graphic designer, typeface designer, and developer working under the name KOCI. With a rich professional background, his expertise spans across multiple disciplines including typography, UI/UX, web design, and print media. His work explores the intersection of geometric structure and modularity, focusing on creating typefaces that function as essential structural elements of visual composition. His debut Google Fonts release, Alien Block, reflects his long-standing commitment to systematic design and his diverse experience in the design industry.
