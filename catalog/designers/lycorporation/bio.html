LY Corporation is one of Japan's largest technology companies, established in October 2023 through the reorganization of group companies including LINE Corporation and Yahoo Japan Corporation. The company operates a wide range of digital services spanning communication, search, media, and commerce. The LINE Seed typeface series was developed as a corporate font to embody the “simplicity” and “friendliness” shared across LY Corporation's services, including the LINE messaging app. Following the global release of its Latin version in 2020, the family has expanded to include Japanese, Korean, Thai, and Traditional Chinese (Taiwan), forming a multilingual type system that supports a cohesive brand experience. Originally created as a corporate typeface, LINE Seed has been made publicly available and is now widely used by a broad range of users across apps, websites, presentations, and printed materials.
