MADType is a type foundry founded by Matthew Desmond. The foundry has contributed several open-source typefaces to Google Fonts, including display and text faces spanning both serif and sans-serif categories.

Matthew Desmond's fonts, such as Abel (a modern condensed sans-serif) and Quantico (a techno-futuristic design), have been available on Google Fonts since 2011-2012, demonstrating early participation in the open-source web font movement.
