Matt McInerney is a type designer and graphic designer based in the United States. He is a co-founder of The League of Moveable Type, one of the first open-source type foundries that provides high-quality, free fonts to the design community.

McInerney is known for his work on popular open-source typefaces and has contributed significantly to making quality typography accessible to designers worldwide. He studied at Tyler School of Art and has worked in both print and digital design.
