Monokrom Skriftforlag is an independent Norwegian type foundry founded in 2012 by Sindre Bremnes and Frode Helland. The foundry produces retail typefaces and custom type design. Its typefaces Satyr and Faunus received a Visuelt Gold Medal in 2013. Geologica, a variable sans-serif designed by Bremnes and Helland, is available on Google Fonts.
