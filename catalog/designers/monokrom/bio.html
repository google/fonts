Monokrom is an independent type foundry based in Oslo, Norway, founded by Frode Helland and Sindre Bremnes. The foundry specializes in creating high-quality retail and custom typefaces with a distinctive Scandinavian design sensibility.

Monokrom's typefaces are known for their careful attention to detail, extensive language support, and thoughtful design that balances functionality with character. The foundry has contributed fonts to Google Fonts and works with international clients on custom typography projects.
