Non Foundry is a type foundry founded in 2019 in Monterrey by Jona Saucedo. Since then, our core philosophy has been “aesthetics + functionality.” Their typefaces combine these two attributes with historical references to communicate meaning beyond words.

Over the years, Non has collaborated on a wide range of projects including MIS - Sound and Image Museum of Ceará (Brazil), Kurimanzutto (Mexico), Hessen Film & Medien (Germany), Yonex (Japan), OH Leuven (Belgium), Herbalife (USA), Grandbrothers (Germany), and many more.
