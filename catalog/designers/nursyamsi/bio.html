Graphic designer & founder of NamelaType based in Indonesia. He loved drawing letters and Arabic calligraphy since he was a child, studied the basic rules of Arabic calligraphy at the Islamic Boarding School and graduated from the Indonesian Institute of the Arts (ISI) Yogyakarta. When he learned about Glyphs.app in 2019, he started designing his own typography, and was enthusiastic about harmonizing Latin and Arabic fonts.
