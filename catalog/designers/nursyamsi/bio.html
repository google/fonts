Nur Syamsi is a graphic designer and founder of NamelaType based in Indonesia. He has been drawing letters and Arabic calligraphy since he was a child, he studied the basic rules of Arabic calligraphy at the Islamic Boarding School and graduated from the Indonesian Institute of the Arts (ISI) Yogyakarta. He started designing his own typefaces in 2019, and has been enthusiastic about harmonizing Latin and Arabic fonts ever since.
