Olga Umpeleva is a type designer who has worked with Brownfox, a type design studio. Her typefaces are known for their playful, decorative character with distinctive personalities.

She designed Sevillana, a headline typeface inspired by the handmade ceramic commemorative plates found on house walls in Andalusia, Spain. She also created Henny Penny, an offbeat display font named after the fairy-tale chicken character, featuring a friendly and playful structure.
