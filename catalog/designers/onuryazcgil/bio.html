Onur Yazıcıgil is a Turkish typographer and type designer based in Istanbul. He holds a PhD in Graphic Design with a specialization in Ottoman Typography History from Mimar Sinan Fine Arts University, an MFA in Visual Communication Design from Purdue University (USA), and a BFA in Graphic Design from Bilkent University in Ankara.

He serves as Assistant Professor of Visual Communication Design at Sabanci University in Istanbul. His academic research focuses on Ottoman typography history. He designed Duru Sans, a low-contrast humanist sans-serif typeface available on Google Fonts since 2011.
