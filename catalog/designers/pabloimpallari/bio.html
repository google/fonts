Pablo Impallari is a type designer based in Rosario, Argentina, known for his motto "For the Love of Letters!" He is an active contributor to the open-source typography community and has designed numerous widely-used typefaces.

Impallari maintains a strong presence in the font design community with over 26 open-source font repositories on GitHub. He has earned recognition as an Arctic Code Vault Contributor and maintains popular tools for font testing and development.
