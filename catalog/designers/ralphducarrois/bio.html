Ralph du Carrois is a German type designer and the founder of Carrois Type Design (now bBox Type GmbH), a type foundry based in Berlin. He is best known for designing Fira Sans and Fira Mono, which were originally commissioned by Mozilla for their Firefox OS project.

Du Carrois studied at the University of Applied Sciences in Berlin (HTW Berlin) and has been active in the type design community for many years. Carrois Type Design has produced numerous typefaces for both corporate clients and open-source projects, contributing significantly to the open-source font ecosystem through collaborations with Google Fonts.
