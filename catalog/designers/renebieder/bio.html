René Bieder is an award-winning type designer and founder of his Berlin-based type foundry, creating tailor-made custom fonts and high-quality typefaces that help brands communicate with clarity, character and confidence. He specialises in bespoke brand typography, developing custom typefaces and wordmarks for national and international clients across a diverse range of industries—including automotive, sports, software, entertainment and food. His work focuses on shaping distinctive typographic voices that define and strengthen brand identity.
