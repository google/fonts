Based in Seoul, Sandoll is a Korean type foundry established in 1984 by Paul Geumho Seok. It was Korea's first digital font foundry and has developed typefaces for Samsung, Apple, Google, LG, and IBM. Sandoll designed the Korean glyphs for Noto Sans and Noto Serif KR in collaboration with Google and Adobe. Its Nanum font series, originally commissioned by Naver, includes Nanum Gothic, Nanum Myeongjo, Nanum Gothic Coding, Nanum Brush Script, and Nanum Pen Script, all available on Google Fonts.
