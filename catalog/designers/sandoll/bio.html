Sandoll is a pioneering Korean type foundry founded in 1984, making it one of the oldest and most influential digital type companies in South Korea. The foundry specializes in Korean Hangul typefaces as well as multilingual font families.

Sandoll has played a crucial role in the development of Korean digital typography, creating numerous typefaces used across print, web, and mobile platforms. The foundry continues to innovate in Korean type design while expanding its offerings to support global typography needs.
