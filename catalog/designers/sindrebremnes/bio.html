Sindre Bremnes is a Norwegian type designer and co-founder of Monokrom, an independent type foundry based in Oslo, Norway. He studied at the Type and Media program at the Royal Academy of Art (KABK) in The Hague, Netherlands.

Together with Frode Helland, Bremnes has helped establish Monokrom as a respected foundry known for carefully crafted typefaces with strong Scandinavian design influences.
