Sindre Bremnes is a Norwegian type designer and co-founder of Monokrom Skriftforlag. Before turning to type design in his late thirties, he worked as a journalist and editor at several Norwegian newspapers, including Universitas and Klassekampen. He is self-taught as a type designer. His debut typefaces Satyr and Faunus, both released in 2012, received a Visuelt Gold Medal in 2013. Geologica, a variable sans-serif he designed with Frode Helland, is available on Google Fonts.
