Sophia Tai is a typeface designer based in the UK. She completed a course in MA Typeface Design at University of Reading where she studied and designed a Latin and Tamil multiscript typeface family. In the same year she was listed under Malee's Women of Typographic Excellence. Soon after, she released her first font Streco, a reversed contrast superfat font on Future Fonts and worked on Foldit, a COLRv1 variable-gradient font for Google Fonts.
