Sudtipos is a renowned type foundry based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, founded in 2002. The foundry has become internationally recognized for its distinctive approach to type design, particularly excelling in script, display, and decorative typefaces that often draw inspiration from Latin American lettering traditions, vintage signage, and mid-century design aesthetics.

The foundry was co-founded by Alejandro Paul (Ale Paul), who serves as its principal designer and creative force. Working alongside collaborators including Joluvian, Diego Giaccone, and others, Sudtipos has built an impressive catalog of award-winning typefaces.
