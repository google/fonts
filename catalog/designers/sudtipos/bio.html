Based in Buenos Aires, Sudtipos is a type foundry established in 2002 by Alejandro Paul, Diego Giaccone, Claudio Pousada, and Ariel Garofalo. The foundry specializes in script and display typefaces for packaging, advertising, and corporate identity. Its catalog includes over 270 typeface families. Fifteen of its fonts are available on Google Fonts, including Aguafina Script, Mr Dafoe, and Bubblegum Sans.
