Szymon Celej is a Polish type designer who runs Hypnotype, a type design studio. He specializes in creating contemporary typefaces with strong visual character and has collaborated with established type foundries and designers.

His typeface Doppio One, available on Google Fonts since 2012, is a robust low-contrast sans-serif with a boxy, contemporary feel designed for both screen use and display sizes. The font was mastered in collaboration with Eben Sorkin of Sorkin Type Co.
