Tobias Whetton is a designer and software engineer originally from London. He studied biomedical engineering whilst co-founding Supernotes, a novel approach to taking short-form notes. In 2023, he begun developing SN Pro, a rounded humanist sans created to improve readability and consistency within Markdown-heavy environments. Based off Nunito, Tobias refined the family’s spacing and proportions, strengthened ligature behavior, and expanded usability across a wide range of digital contexts. Now he’s living in San Francisco, California and focuses on designing and building ai-native knowledge tools that help us think better.
