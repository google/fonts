Travis Kochel is a co-founder and owner of Future Fonts, where he plays a key role in the development of the influential platform. He is also the lead type designer and director for Vectro, overseeing the design and development of typefaces. As a partner at Scribble Tone for the past decade, he has focused on type design and development, and is best known for his award-winning typeface, Chartwell. In addition to his design work, Travis is an Adjunct Professor at Portland State University, where he teaches Type Design.
