TypeHabesha is an independent digital type foundry based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, specializing in multi-script branding and contemporary type design. Founded by Behailu Berento, the studio focuses on bridging the gap between traditional script aesthetics and modern typographic standards, with a core expertise in Ethiopic (Ge'ez) and Latin writing systems. TypeHabesha is committed to creating authentic, highly legible, and culturally rich open-source font families that empower local and international creators across the modern digital landscape.
