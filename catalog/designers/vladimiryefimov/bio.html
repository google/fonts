Vladimir Yefimov (1949-2012) was a prominent Russian type designer and one of the founders of ParaType, the leading Russian digital type foundry. He was instrumental in developing high-quality Cyrillic typefaces and adapting Western designs for Russian typography.

Yefimov designed numerous original typefaces and supervised the development of many more at ParaType. His work helped establish modern standards for Cyrillic type design and made professional typography accessible to Russian-speaking designers and publishers.
