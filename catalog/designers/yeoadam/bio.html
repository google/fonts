Adam Yeo is a graphic and type designer from Côte d'Ivoire. He holds a Doctorate in Art and Design from Nanjing University of the Arts, China. From 2022 to 2024, Adam was part of the ANRT, where he worked on developing a typeface for the Bété script. Upon returning to his home country, he was appointed as an Assistant Professor of Graphic Design at the University of Bondoukou, Côte d'Ivoire.

Adam has published several articles on African scripts, symbols, and languages. Matemasie is his first Latin typeface.
