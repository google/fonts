Erin is an independent typeface designer who specializes in Indian writing systems. Since 2013, she has worked as a non-Latin type consultant and type designer for clients such as Adobe, Facebook, Google, TypeTogether, Frere-Jones Type and other independent foundries. She previously worked at Hoefler & Frere-Jones and received an MA in Typeface Design from the University of Reading. Erin also collects Indic type and lettering images on her blog Hindi Rinny.
