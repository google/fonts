Fantoms is a retro display font inspired by 1950s/60s music posters and band lettering. It was created for The Fantoms by Gary Daggers from original drawings, then digitised for public release.

Fantoms is ideal for music branding, show cards, vintage promo art, and titles that need a lively rock’n’roll feel.
