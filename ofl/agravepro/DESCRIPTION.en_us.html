Agràve Pro is a carefully crafted, cheerful script typeface, designed especially for setting large bodies of text. With its open counters, short ascenders and descenders, consistent stroke width, and over 6,500 kerning pairs, it offers outstanding readability.

The Agràve Pro font family includes three weights: Light, Regular and Bold. It features 783 glyphs, an extensive set of ligatures, and full multilingual support. Every accented character is uniquely drawn, adding subtle variation and character. The family also includes a complete set of multilingual small caps and an impressive range of numeral styles: lining, oldstyle, tabular, fractions, and scientific inferiors.

The source files for this font family are available on GitHub: https://github.com/daanjobsis/fonts
