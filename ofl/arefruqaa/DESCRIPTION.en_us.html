Aref Ruqaa is an Arabic typeface that aspires to capture the essence of the classical Ruqaa calligraphic style. It is a highly legible and readable typeface. Perfect for both screen and print usage.

The Latin part is based on AMS Euler, but spaced for regular text rather than mathematics.

The Aref Ruqaa project is led by Khaled Hosny, a type designer based in Cairo, Egypt. To contribute, see github.com/alif-type/aref-ruqaa
