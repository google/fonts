People read and interact with text on screens more and more each day. What happens on screen ends up being more important than what comes out of the printer.

Bitter is a contemporary slab serif typeface specially designed for comfortable on-screen reading. It combines the large x-heights and legibility of the humanistic tradition with subtle characteristics in the characters that inject a certain rhythm to flowing texts.

The typeface is balanced and manually spaced. It is legible, with a visual character that works well for fashion and blog writing, portfolio content, apps, or any use-case that involves marketing.
