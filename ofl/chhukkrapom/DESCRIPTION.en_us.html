Chhuk Krapom

Chhuk Krapom is a Khmer script typeface designed by KONG Rotana. This font features a modern and clean design, making it suitable for both display and body text in digital and print media.

The typeface is crafted with smooth curves and balanced strokes, ensuring readability and aesthetic appeal. Chhuk Krapom is ideal for web design, branding, publishing, and UI/UX interfaces.
