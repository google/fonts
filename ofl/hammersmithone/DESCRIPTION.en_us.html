Hammersmith One is a very low contrast typeface inspired by the Johnston UK lettering tradition. Hammersmith One shows the quirks of a somewhat naive, handmade, brush written letters including a wider than normal "e" and "s", as well as dark joins between strokes which are normally compensated for in type. The sources for this design have been adapted not just for type but specifically for use as a web type. This font works well for even smaller sizes than was originally expected.
