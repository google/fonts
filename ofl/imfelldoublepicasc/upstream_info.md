## Designer

Igino Marini (iginomarini.com). Copyright: "(c) 2007 Igino Marini (www.iginomarini.com mail@iginomarini.com)". The family is a small caps variant of IM Fell Double Pica. The single font file is `IMFeDPsc28P.ttf` (version 3.00).

## librefonts Mirror

A mirror exists at `https://github.com/librefonts/imfelldoublepicasc` with a single commit:
- `2274064` (2014-10-17) -- "update .travis.yml"

The repo contains only TTX-disassembled font data (no VFB, SFD, or other editable source files). Like the non-SC variant, this is an archival mirror of the compiled TTF, not the original design source. The SC variant was originally provided alongside the other IM Fell variants from Igino Marini's personal website.
