Jost is an original font created by indestructible type*. It is inspired by 1920s German sans-serifs. This is version 3.5. Jost is designed and maintained by Owen Earl, who is the creator of the font foundry indestructible type*. in 2020 Owen Earl, and Mirko Velimirovic worked together to make Jost a variable font.

If you have questions or want to help out, please contribute at github.com/indestructible-type/Jost.
