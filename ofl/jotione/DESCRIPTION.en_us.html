Joti One was inspired by Eduardo Tunni’s little son – Jonah, who is called "Joti" by his friends. The type has the informality and style of cartoons children love to watch. Its primary concept is top-heavy stems with a slight sense of movement to further enhance the style. Joti is ideal for composing headlines and short texts in sizes larger than 14 points.

To contribute to the project contact Eduardo Tunni.
