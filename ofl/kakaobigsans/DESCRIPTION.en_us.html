Kakao Big Sans (Korean : 카카오 큰글씨) are unicode fonts designed especially for the Korean-language script, designed by FONTRIX Inc. (Korean : 폰트릭스) & Min Bon (Korean : 민본) & Choi Min-gyu (Korean : 최민규). The publisher is Kakao. Kakao Big Sans is a typeface designed for digital environments, featuring bold shapes that make it ideal for both headlines and body text.
