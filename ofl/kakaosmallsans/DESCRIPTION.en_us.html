Kakao Small Sans (Korean : 카카오 작은글씨) are unicode fonts designed especially for the Korean-language script, designed by FONTRIX Inc. (Korean : 폰트릭스) & Min Bon (Korean : 민본) & Choi Min-gyu (Korean : 최민규). The publisher is Kakao. Kakao Small Sans is a typeface designed for digital environments, featuring structural details that enhance readability, making it ideal for body text.
