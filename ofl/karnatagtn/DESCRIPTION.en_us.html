Karnata GTN is a variable Kannada typeface inspired by the handwriting of G. T. Narayan Rao. It explores a balance between expressive handwritten forms and structured typographic clarity.

The typeface was developed by studying original handwritten material and translating it into a functional digital system. It supports Kannada and Latin scripts, with a variable weight axis ranging from 400 to 900.

Karnata GTN is designed for modern digital usage, including user interfaces, publishing, and software environments requiring robust Kannada text rendering.
