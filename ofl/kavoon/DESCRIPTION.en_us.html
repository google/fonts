Kavoon is a display typeface based on experiments with brush and ink. Kavoon's expressive features make words vivid and powerfully draw the reader in. It is an eloquent and friendly font that can be used from medium to large sizes for headers, posters or menus.

To contribute to the project, visit github.com/EbenSorkin/Kavoon

Updated: February 2016, to v1.004 with additional language support, improved hinting, and other minor fixes.
