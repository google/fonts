The austere and concise personality and flexibility of Ledger, makes it a real multiple-purpose typeface. The letter forms are distinguished by a large x-height, sufficient stroke contrast, robust but elegant wedge-like serifs and terminals. The typeface can be qualified as matched to modern trends of type design and of enhanced legibility and readability, which makes it favorable for print and display processes - such as in newspapers and magazines, corporate ID, advertising, laser printed documents and on low resolution screens.
