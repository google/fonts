Noto Fangsong KSS Vertical is a design for the historical East Asian Khitan small script script.

Noto Fangsong KSS Vertical contains 1,917 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 497 characters from 3 Unicode blocks: Khitan Small Script, CJK Unified Ideographs, Basic Latin.
