Noto Sans Gunjala Gondi is a design for the Indic Gunjala Gondi script.

Noto Sans Gunjala Gondi has multiple weights, contains 254 glyphs, 6 OpenType features, and supports 94 characters from 3 Unicode blocks: Gunjala Gondi, Basic Latin, General Punctuation.
