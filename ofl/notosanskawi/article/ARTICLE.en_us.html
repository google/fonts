Noto Sans Kawi is a design for the historical Southeast Asian Kawi script.

Noto Sans Kawi has multiple weights, contains 110 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 88 characters from the Unicode block Kawi.

Supported writing systems

Kawi

Kawi is a historical Southeast Asian abugida, written left-to-right. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, r12a.
