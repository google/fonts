Noto Sans Multani is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the historical Indic Multani script.

Noto Sans Multani contains 53 glyphs, and supports 52 characters from the Unicode block Multani.

Supported writing systems

Multani

Multani (𑊠𑊣𑊖𑊚) is a historical Indic abjad. Was used in the 18th–20th century in today’s India and Pakistan for the Saraiki language, mainly by merchants. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, Wiktionary, r12a.
