Noto Sans Oriya is an unmodulated (“sans serif”) design for texts in the Indic Odia (Oriya) script.

Noto Sans Oriya contains 513 glyphs, 19 OpenType features, and supports 150 characters from 3 Unicode blocks: Oriya, Basic Latin, General Punctuation.

Supported writing systems

Odia (Oriya)

Odia (Oriya, ଉତ୍କଳ ) is an Indic abugida, written left-to-right (21 million users). Used since the c. 14th century in India for the Odia language (state language of Orissa). Also used for Dravidian and Munda languages. Needs software support for complex text layout (shaping). Read more on ScriptSource , Unicode , Wikipedia , Wiktionary , r12a .
