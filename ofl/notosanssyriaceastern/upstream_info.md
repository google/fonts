## Summary

Noto Sans Syriac Eastern was sourced from the notofonts/syriac repository (shared with Noto Sans Syriac and Noto Sans Syriac Western). The family was published as a variable font with a `wght` axis (100–900), covering the Eastern Syriac script variant (Syrc) and supporting Assyrian Neo-Aramaic, Classical Syriac, Syriac, Turoyo, and Arabic written in Syriac. Files were built using the gftools-builder pipeline with `sources/config-sans-syriac-eastern.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2023-01-06 and released at version 3.001.
