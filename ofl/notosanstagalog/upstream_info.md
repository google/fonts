## Summary

Noto Sans Tagalog was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tagalog GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tagalog (Baybayin) script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Filipino in the Tagalog script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
