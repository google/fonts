## Summary

Noto Sans Tagbanwa was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tagbanwa GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tagbanwa script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Sanskrit and Tagbanwa (tbw) in the Tagbanwa script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
