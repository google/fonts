## Summary

Noto Sans Tai Le was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tai-le GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tai Le script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Dehong Dai (tdd) in the Tai Le script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
