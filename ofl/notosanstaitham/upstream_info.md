## Summary

Noto Sans Tai Tham was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tai-tham GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with a weight axis (400–700) covering the Tai Tham (Lanna) script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Khün (kkh) and Northern Thai (nod) in the Lanna script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
