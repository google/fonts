## Summary

Noto Sans Tai Viet was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tai-viet GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tai Viet script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Black Tai (blt) in the Tai Viet script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
