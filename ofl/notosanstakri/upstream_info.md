## Summary

Noto Sans Takri was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/takri GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Takri script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Dogri (doi) in the Takri script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
