## Summary

Noto Sans Tamil was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tamil GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with width (62.5–100) and weight (100–900) axes covering the Tamil script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Badaga (bfq) and Tamil (ta) in the Tamil script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
