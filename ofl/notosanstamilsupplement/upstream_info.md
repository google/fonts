## Summary

Noto Sans Tamil Supplement was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tamil GitHub repository (shared with Noto Sans Tamil). The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tamil Supplement Unicode block alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Badaga (bfq) and Tamil (ta). The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
