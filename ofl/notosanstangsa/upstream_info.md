## Summary

Noto Sans Tangsa was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tangsa GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with a weight axis (400–700) covering the Tangsa script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Naga, Tangsa (nst) in the Tangsa script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2022-09-12.
