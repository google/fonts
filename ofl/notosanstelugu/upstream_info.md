## Summary

Noto Sans Telugu was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/telugu GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with width (62.5–100) and weight (100–900) axes covering the Telugu script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Gondi (gon), Lambadi (lmn), Telugu (te), and Waddar (wbq) in the Telugu script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
