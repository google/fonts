## Summary

Noto Sans Thaana was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/thaana GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with a weight axis (100–900) covering the Thaana script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Dhivehi (dv) in the Thaana script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
