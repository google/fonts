## Summary

Noto Sans Thai was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/thai GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with width (62.5–100) and weight (100–900) axes covering the Thai script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Kuy (kdt), Northern Khmer (kxm), Western Lawa (lcp), Eastern Lawa (lwl), Pali (pi), Southern Thai (sou), Thai (th), and Northeastern Thai (tts). The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
