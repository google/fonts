## Summary

Noto Sans Thai Looped was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/thai GitHub repository (shared with Noto Sans Thai). The family provided a variable font with width (62.5–100) and weight (100–900) axes covering the Thai script in looped style alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Kuy (kdt), Northern Khmer (kxm), Western Lawa (lcp), Eastern Lawa (lwl), Pali (pi), Southern Thai (sou), Thai (th), and Northeastern Thai (tts). The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
