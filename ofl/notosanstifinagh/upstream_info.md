## Summary

Noto Sans Tifinagh was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tifinagh GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tifinagh script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Berber (ber), Kabyle (kab), Tarifit (rif), Tachelhit (shi), Tamasheq (taq), Central Atlas Tamazight (tzm), Zenaga (zen), and Standard Moroccan Tamazight (zgh) in the Tifinagh script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
