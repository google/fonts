## Summary

Noto Sans Tirhuta was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/tirhuta GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Tirhuta (Mithilakshar) script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Maithili (mai) and Sanskrit (sa) in the Tirhuta script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
