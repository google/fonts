## Summary

Noto Sans Ugaritic was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/ugaritic GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Ugaritic script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Assyrian Neo-Aramaic (aii) and Ugaritic (uga) in the Ugaritic script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
