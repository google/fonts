## Summary

Noto Sans Vithkuqi was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/vithkuqi GitHub repository. The family provided a variable font with a weight axis (400–700) covering the Vithkuqi script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Albanian (sq) in the Vithkuqi script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2022-10-11.
