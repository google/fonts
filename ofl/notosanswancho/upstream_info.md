## Summary

Noto Sans Wancho was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/wancho GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Wancho script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Wancho Naga (nnp) and Sanskrit (sa) in the Wancho script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
