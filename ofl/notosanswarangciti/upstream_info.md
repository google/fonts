## Summary

Noto Sans Warang Citi was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/warang-citi GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Warang Citi script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Ho (hoc) and Sanskrit (sa) in the Warang Citi script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
