## Summary

Noto Sans Yi was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/yi GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Yi script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Sichuan Yi (ii) in the Yi script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
