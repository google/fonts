## Summary

Noto Sans Zanabazar Square was designed by Google and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the notofonts/zanabazar-square GitHub repository. The family provided a single Regular style covering the Zanabazar Square script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets, supporting Tibetan (bo), Mongolian (mn), and Sanskrit (sa) in the Zanabazar Square script. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-11-19.
