Noto Serif, like its sister Sans family, is intended to be visually harmonious across multiple languages, with compatible heights and stroke thicknesses. Noto has a functional look with a warm touch and is ideal for magazines, editorial and publishing. For more information about Noto, go to google.com/get/noto

This is the Serif Latin, Greek and Cyrillic and Devanagari family. It has Regular, Bold, Italic and Bold Italic styles and is hinted. It has a Sans-Serif sister family, Noto Sans.

Noto fonts for many other languages are available as web fonts in Google Fonts Early Access.
