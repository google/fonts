## Font Details

Orbit is a Korean/Latin typeface designed by Sooun Cho for JAMO. It is inspired by monospaced Latin coding fonts, with Korean characters (Hangeul) and punctuation given a mathematical and geometric character through right-angle serifs and orbicular circles. The design uses symmetry and connectivity to create a futuristic, cosmic atmosphere. The font is recommended for use at small sizes with light text on dark backgrounds.

The Google Fonts version ships a single static TTF: `Orbit-Regular.ttf`, sourced from `Fonts/ttf/` in the upstream repository.
