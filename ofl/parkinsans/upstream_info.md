## Font Description

Parkinsans is a sans-serif variable font with a weight range from Light (300) to ExtraBold (800). The font was designed in support of Parkinson's disease awareness and advocacy; its design features are informed by research on legibility for people with Parkinson's disease. The name is a combination of "Parkinson" and "sans."
