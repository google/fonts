## Font Description

Pixelify Sans is a pixel-art-inspired display sans-serif variable font designed by Stefie Justprince. The font evokes the aesthetic of early computer and video game typography while maintaining legibility as a functional typeface. It supports a weight range from Regular (400) to Bold (700). The font covers Latin, Latin Extended, and Cyrillic scripts. The copyright dates to 2021.
