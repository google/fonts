## Summary

Playwrite GB S Guides (display name: Playwrite England SemiJoined Guides) was designed by TypeTogether (Veronika Burian, José Scaglione) and published under the OFL. It was sourced from the TypeTogether/Playwrite GitHub repository, a monorepo containing all Playwrite regional variants. The family provided static Regular and Italic fonts with handwriting guide overlays, along with documentation and article files for the GB SemiJoined style. Files were sourced from `fonts/ttf/` and `documentation/about-guides/GB_S/` in the repository. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2024-11-27.
