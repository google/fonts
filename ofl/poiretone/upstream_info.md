## Font Description

Poiret One is a decorative geometric grotesque display typeface designed by Denis Masharov. The design carries a strong Art Deco and constructivist aesthetic with light forms and elegant styling, based on geometric forms with stylish lines and graceful curves. It was added to Google Fonts on 2012-02-29.

The typeface was described as suitable for large signs, labels, titles, headlines, and general graphic design work — in motion graphics, on the web, and in print. Despite being a single-weight display face, it also supported lowercase letters, making it useful for all-caps as well as mixed-case typography.
