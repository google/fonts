## Font Description

Protest Revolution is a display typeface and part of the Protest Types project by designer Octavio Pardo. The project began as a personal exploration of the shapes and voices of protest signage. Protest Revolution expressed the furious and messy appearance of painted protest signs.

Unlike Strike, Guerrilla, and Riot — which shared identical spacing and kerning — Protest Revolution had a distinct, more expressive character that diverged from the uniform-spacing system of its siblings.

The font was added to Google Fonts on 2023-12-13.
