PT Sans is a typeface that can stand on its own, and blend in seamlessly. PT Sans is based on Russian sans serif types of the second part of the 20th century, but at the same time has distinctive features of contemporary humanistic designs. The family consists of 8 basic styles, which satisfy the need for flexibility, creating wholesome variations of texture and emphasis. It is suitable for branding and corporate identities

Designed by Alexandra Korolkova, Olga Umpeleva and Vladimir Yefimov and released by ParaType in 2009.
