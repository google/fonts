## Notes

PT Sans Caption is the small-text optical size variant of PT Sans, designed for use at small point sizes (captions, footnotes). It shares the same design heritage as PT Sans — commissioned by the Federal Agency on Press and Mass Communications of the Russian Federation, designed by Alexandra Korolkova under Paratype leadership, released in 2009.
