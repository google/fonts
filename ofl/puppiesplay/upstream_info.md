## Font Description

Puppies Play is a fun, bouncy script typeface designed by Robert Leuschke. It was added to Google Fonts on 2021-10-13, on the same date as the designer's other font, Praise. The design featured connectors that gave the letters a playful flow, and was intended for lighthearted applications such as baby shower invitations, nursery rhymes, and children's design work.

The font included Latin character sets covering Western European, Central European, and Vietnamese languages.
