Puritan is a sans serif typeface made in response to Ben Weiner being influenced by the techniques for making grotesques, geometric and humanist sans serifs, and a typeface called Ehrhardt; a Monotype revival typeface based on a seventeenth century design by Miklos Kis. Puritan is legible in long body text and is suitable for newspapers, handbooks and articles.

Ben Weiner wrote a short essay about Puritan as a project in his final year as an undergraduate, available for download as a PDF file
