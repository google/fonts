Readex Pro is the world-script expansion of the Lexend fonts. Readex Pro is designed by Thomas Jockin and Nadine Chahine and currently supports Latin and Arabic.

Lexend is a family of variable fonts designed by Bonnie Shaver-Troup, Thomas Jockin and Font Bureau. Applying the Shaver-Troup Individually Optimal Text Formation Factors, studies have found readers instantaneously improve their reading fluency.

The fonts are based on the Quicksand project from Andrew Paglinawan. To contribute, see github.com/ThomasJockin/readexpro.
