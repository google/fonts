Rounded Cutter

Designer: Davide Mura

Foundry: The RoundedCutter Project

License: SIL Open Font License 1.1 (OFL)

About the font

Rounded Cutter is a modern sans-serif typeface designed for readability and a friendly appearance. It includes a full set of Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese characters. The design emphasizes even weight distribution and legibility at both small and large sizes.

Features

  • Weights: Light, Regular, Medium, Bold
  • Styles: Roman and Italic
  • Support for accented characters in European and Vietnamese languages
  • OpenType features: aalt, ccmp, frac, locl, ordn, sinf, subs, sups, kern, mark, mkmk

Use

Ideal for editorial design, digital design, user interfaces, and digital reading. Rounded Cutter is suitable for both display and text sizes, providing clarity and consistency.

History

The typeface was developed in 2025 by Davide Mura, using Glyphs 3.3.1 and optimized through iterative testing with FontBakery and manual proofing. Particular attention was paid to optical balance and the design of accented characters.
