Rowdies is a Latin display typeface inspired by the rough & tough Indian action cinema.

Designed for Action, Drama, Thriller, Noir, Crime, Adventure genres. The font family includes 3 weights - Light: designed to set supporting body-text with optimal glyphs width. Regular: designed for the large headers & titles. Bold: designed for extremely heavyweight title designs.

Rowdies is designed by Jaikishan Patel. To contribute, see github.com/magictype/rowdy
