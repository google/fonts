## Summary

Signika was designed by Anna Giedryś and hosted under the googlefonts GitHub organization. The family was published as a variable font with two axes: GRAD (Grade, -30 to 0) and wght (Weight, 300 to 700). It supported Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The same repository also served as the source for Signika SC and Signika Negative, with build configuration at `sources/config.yaml`.
