## Summary

Signika Negative was designed by Anna Giedryś. The Google Fonts build was sourced from a fork maintained by Aaron Bell at github.com/aaronbell/signika, which provided the negative (ink-trap) variant of the Signika family. The family was published as a variable font with a single wght axis (300 to 700). It supported Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The build configuration was located at `sources/config.yaml`.
