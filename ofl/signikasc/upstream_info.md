## Summary

Signika SC was designed by Anna Giedryś as the small-capitals variant of the Signika family. It was sourced from the same repository as Signika, with the build driven by the config at `sources/config.yaml`. The family was published as a variable font with a single wght axis (300 to 700). It supported Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets.
