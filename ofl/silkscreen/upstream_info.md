## Summary

Silkscreen was originally designed by Jason Kottke in 2001 and later hosted under the googlefonts GitHub organization. The family consisted of two static TTF weights: Regular and Bold. It supported Latin and Latin Extended subsets. The repository included a build configuration at `sources/config.yaml`.
