## Summary

Sixtyfour was designed by Jens Kutílek as part of the homecomputer-fonts project, inspired by the bitmap display of the Commodore 64. The family was published as a variable font with two custom axes: BLED (Bleed, 0 to 100) and SCAN (Scanlines, -53 to 100). It supported Latin, Latin Extended, Math, and Symbols subsets. The build configuration was at `Sixtyfour/sources/config.yaml` within the monorepo. A demo minisite was available at jenskutilek.github.io/homecomputer-fonts.
