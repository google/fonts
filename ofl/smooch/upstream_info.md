## Summary

Smooch was designed by Robert Leuschke and hosted under the googlefonts GitHub organization. The family consisted of a single Regular weight static TTF in the Handwriting/Display category. It supported Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The build configuration was at `sources/config.yml`.
