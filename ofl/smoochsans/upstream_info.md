## Summary

Smooch Sans was designed by Robert Leuschke and hosted under the googlefonts GitHub organization. The family was published as a variable font with a single wght axis spanning the full range from Thin (100) to Black (900). It supported Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The build configuration was at `sources/config.yml`.
