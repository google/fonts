## Summary

Sofia Sans was designed by Lettersoup (Botio Nikoltchev and Ani Petrova) and maintained in the lettersoup GitHub organization. The family was published as a variable font pair (upright and italic) with a wght axis spanning an ultra-wide range of 1 to 1000. It supported Cyrillic, Cyrillic Extended, Greek, Latin, and Latin Extended subsets. The build configuration for the normal width variant was at `sources/Normal/sofia-normal.yml`. A project minisite was available at lettersoup.de/sofia-sans/. The same repository also served as the source for Sofia Sans Condensed, Sofia Sans Semi Condensed, and Sofia Sans Extra Condensed.
