## Summary

Sofia Sans Semi Condensed was designed by Lettersoup (Botio Nikoltchev and Ani Petrova) as the semi-condensed width variant of the Sofia Sans family. It was sourced from the same repository as Sofia Sans. The family was published as a variable font pair (upright and italic) with a wght axis spanning 1 to 1000. It supported Cyrillic, Cyrillic Extended, Greek, Latin, and Latin Extended subsets. The build configuration was at `sources/Semi-Condensed/sofia-semi-cond.yml`.
