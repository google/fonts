## Summary

Solitreo was designed by Nathan Gross and Bryan Kirschen under the Ladino Projects initiative, dedicated to the preservation and digitization of Judeo-Spanish (Ladino) written heritage. The font reproduced the traditional Solitreo cursive script used by Sephardic Jewish communities for writing Ladino. The primary script was Hebrew (Hebr). It supported Hebrew, Latin, and Latin Extended subsets. The build configuration was at `sources/config.yaml`.
