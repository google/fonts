## Summary

Sometype Mono was designed by Ryoichi Tsunekawa and hosted under the googlefonts GitHub organization. The family was published as a variable font pair (upright and italic) with a wght axis spanning Regular (400) to Bold (700). It supported Latin and Latin Extended subsets. The build configuration was at `sources/config.yaml`.
