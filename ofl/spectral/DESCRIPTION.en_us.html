Spectral is a new and versatile serif face available in seven weights of roman and italic, with small caps. Spectral offers an efficient, beautiful design that is intended primarily for text-rich, screen-first environments and long-form reading.

There's also Spectral SC, the small cap version of this type.

Brought to you by Production Type and commissioned by Google Fonts, Spectral is now free to use across Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides, or in any of your projects. To contribute, see github.com/productiontype/spectral
