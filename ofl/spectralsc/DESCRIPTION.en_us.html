Spectral SC is the small caps version of Spectral - a new and versatile serif face available in seven weights of roman and italic, with small caps.

Spectral offers an efficient, beautiful design that is intended primarily for text-rich, screen-first environments and long-form reading. Brought to you by Production Type and commissioned by Google Fonts, Spectral is now free to use across Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides, or in any of your projects.
