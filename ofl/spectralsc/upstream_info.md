## Font Files

The family was published as 14 static fonts covering seven weights (ExtraLight through ExtraBold) in upright and italic variants, all featuring small capitals:

- `SpectralSC-ExtraLight.ttf`, `SpectralSC-ExtraLightItalic.ttf`
- `SpectralSC-Light.ttf`, `SpectralSC-LightItalic.ttf`
- `SpectralSC-Regular.ttf`, `SpectralSC-Italic.ttf`
- `SpectralSC-Medium.ttf`, `SpectralSC-MediumItalic.ttf`
- `SpectralSC-SemiBold.ttf`, `SpectralSC-SemiBoldItalic.ttf`
- `SpectralSC-Bold.ttf`, `SpectralSC-BoldItalic.ttf`
- `SpectralSC-ExtraBold.ttf`, `SpectralSC-ExtraBoldItalic.ttf`

## Subsets

The family covered the following subsets: cyrillic, cyrillic-ext, latin, latin-ext, vietnamese.

## Notes

Spectral SC was designed by Production Type and published to Google Fonts on 2017-10-10. It was the small capitals companion to the Spectral family, sharing the same upstream repository. The SC fonts resided in a dedicated `fonts/ttf/SC/` subdirectory within the repository.
