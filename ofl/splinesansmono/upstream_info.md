## Font Files

The family was published as two variable fonts covering the weight (`wght`) axis:

- `SplineSansMono[wght].ttf` — upright
- `SplineSansMono-Italic[wght].ttf` — italic

## Subsets

The family covered the following subsets: latin, latin-ext.

## Notes

Spline Sans Mono was designed by Eben Sorkin and Mirko Velimirović and published to Google Fonts on 2022-03-28. It was the monospace companion to Spline Sans, hosted in a separate repository.
