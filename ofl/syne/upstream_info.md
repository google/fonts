## Notes

Syne was designed by Bonjour Monde, Lucas Descroix, and George Triantafyllakos and published to Google Fonts on 2020-08-25. Notably, the upstream repository was hosted on GitLab rather than GitHub. The family offered Greek script support in addition to Latin coverage.
