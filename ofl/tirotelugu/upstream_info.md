## Summary

Tiro Telugu was designed by Tiro Typeworks (John Hudson, Fiona Ross, Kaja Słojewska) and published under the OFL. It was part of the Indigo project hosted at the TiroTypeworks/Indigo GitHub repository. The family provided Regular and Italic styles covering the Telugu script alongside Latin and Latin Extended subsets. Files were sourced from the `fonts/TiroTelugu/TTF/` directory of the repository. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2022-03-30.
