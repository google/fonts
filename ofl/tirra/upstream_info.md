## Summary

Tirra was designed by SIL International and published under the OFL. It was developed for the Tifinagh script, used by Amazigh (Berber) language communities. The family provided six weights (Regular through Black: 400–900) without italic styles. Files were sourced from the v4.100 release archive of the font-akatab repository maintained by SIL's Regional Scripts Initiative (SILNRSI). The Tifinagh-script sample text reads "ⴽⵓ ⵢⴰⵏ ⴷⴰⵔⵙ ⵓⵣⵔⴼ ⵏ ⵓⵙⴳⵎⵉ" (Every person has the right to education). The font was added to Google Fonts on 2025-07-18.
