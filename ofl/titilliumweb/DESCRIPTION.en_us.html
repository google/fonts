Titillium Web’s is a semi modular sans serif with a mildly rounded finish. It is a largely geometric typeface, however its rounded finish gives it a warm look. This makes Titillium Web suitable for both formal and free- range projects.

Three years after the birth of Titillium, the project is still evolving, and even we don’t know what it will become in the future.
