## Summary

Tomorrow was designed by Tony de Marco and Monica Rizzolli and published under the OFL. It was a geometric sans-serif display family providing nine weights (Thin through Black) in both upright and italic styles, totalling 18 font files. The family covered Latin and Latin Extended subsets. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2019-10-02.
