## Summary

Tourney was designed by Tyler Finck of Etcetera Type Co. and published under the OFL. It was a variable display font family with two variable axes: weight (wght, 100–900) and width (wdth, 75–125), providing upright and italic variable fonts. It covered Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. Variable font binaries were sourced from `fonts/variable/` and the build configuration from `Sources/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2021-04-29.
