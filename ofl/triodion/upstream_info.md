## Summary

Triodion was designed by Aleksandr Andreev and published under the OFL. It was a Cyrillic display typeface created for Church Slavonic liturgical texts, covering Cyrillic, Cyrillic Extended, and Latin subsets. Its primary script was Cyrillic and its primary language was Church Slavonic (`cu_Cyrl`). The Regular weight TTF was sourced from `fonts/ttf/` and the build configuration from `sources/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2024-12-06.
