## Summary

Trocchi was designed by Vernon Adams and published under the OFL. It was a serif display typeface providing a single Regular weight covering Latin and Latin Extended subsets. The TTF file was sourced from `fonts/ttf/` in the repository maintained by Marc Foley (m4rc1e). The font was added to Google Fonts on 2012-04-04.
