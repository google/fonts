## Summary

Unbounded was designed by NaN and published under the OFL. It was a variable sans-serif typeface with a single weight axis (wght, 200–900) covering Cyrillic, Cyrillic Extended, Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The font was created in association with the Polkadot network and had a minisite at https://unbounded.polkadot.network/. The variable TTF was sourced from `fonts/variable/` and the build configuration from `Source/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2022-11-07.
