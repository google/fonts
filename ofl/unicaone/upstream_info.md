## Summary

Unica One was designed by Eduardo Tunni and published under the OFL. It was a sans-serif display typeface providing a single Regular weight covering Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The TTF file was sourced from `fonts/ttf/` in the repository. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2012-10-26.
