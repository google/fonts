## Summary

Unlock was designed by Eduardo Tunni and published under the OFL with the Reserved Font Name "Unlock". It was a serif display typeface providing a single Regular weight covering Latin and Latin Extended subsets. The TTF file was sourced from `fonts/ttf/` in the repository. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2011-11-30.
