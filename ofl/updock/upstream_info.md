## Summary

Updock was designed by Robert Leuschke and published under the OFL. It was a handwriting/display typeface providing a single Regular weight covering Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The TTF was sourced from `fonts/ttf/` in the googlefonts/updock repository, with the build configuration at `sources/config.yml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2022-03-24.
