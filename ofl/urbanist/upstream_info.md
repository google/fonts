## Summary

Urbanist was designed by Corey Hu and published under the OFL. It was a geometric sans-serif variable display typeface with a single weight axis (wght, 100–900) provided in upright and italic variable fonts, covering Latin and Latin Extended subsets. Variable TTF binaries were sourced from `fonts/variable/` and the build configuration from `sources/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2021-06-02.
