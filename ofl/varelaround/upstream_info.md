## Summary

Varela Round was designed by Joe Prince and published under the OFL with Reserved Font Names "Varela" and "Varela Round". It was a rounded sans-serif typeface providing a single Regular weight covering Hebrew, Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The TTF was sourced from `fonts/ttf/` in the m4rc1e/Varela-Round-Hebrew repository (a mastering fork of the original alefalefalef/Varela-Round-Hebrew), from the `gf-mastering` branch with build configuration at `sources/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2011-07-13.
