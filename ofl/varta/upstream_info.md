## Summary

Varta was designed by Joana Correia, Viktoriya Grabowska, and Eben Sorkin, and published under the OFL. It was a sans-serif variable typeface with a single weight axis (wght, 300–700) covering Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The font was hosted in the SorkinType/Varta repository. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2020-06-11.
