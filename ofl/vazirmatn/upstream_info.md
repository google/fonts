## Summary

Vazirmatn was designed by Saber Rastikerdar and published under the OFL. It was a sans-serif variable typeface for the Arabic script with a single weight axis (wght, 100–900), covering Arabic, Latin, and Latin Extended subsets. The variable TTF was sourced from `fonts/variable/` in the repository. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2022-03-16.
