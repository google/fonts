## Summary

Vend Sans was designed by Bloom Type Foundry (Baptiste Guesnon) and published under the OFL. It was a sans-serif variable typeface with a single weight axis (wght, 300–700) provided in upright and italic variable fonts, covering Latin and Latin Extended subsets. Variable TTF binaries were sourced from `fonts/variable/` and the build configuration from `sources/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2025-06-06.
