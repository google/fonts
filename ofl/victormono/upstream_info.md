## Summary

Victor Mono was designed by Rune Bjørnerås and published under the OFL. It was a monospace variable typeface with a weight axis (wght, 100–700) provided in upright and italic variable fonts, covering Cyrillic, Cyrillic Extended, Greek, Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. Files were sourced from the v1.561 release archive. The font had a minisite at https://rubjo.github.io/victor-mono. The build configuration was at `sources/config.yaml`. The font was added to Google Fonts on 2023-06-21.
