Xanh Mono is a mono-serif typeface, designed by Lam Bao and Duy Dao from the first digital type foundry in Vietnam, also known as Yellow Type Foundry. In Vietnamese, “Xanh” has a lot of meanings, including blue; green; young; etc. We believe that Xanh Mono will not only present a fresh and gentle look, but also stylish and unique for both reading and display purpose.

To contribute see https://github.com/yellow-type-foundry/xanhmono.
