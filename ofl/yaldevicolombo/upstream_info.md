## Summary

Yaldevi Colombo was designed by Mooniak (with contributions by Sol Matas) as a static sans-serif typeface supporting Latin and Sinhala scripts. The fonts were sourced from the `mooniak/yaldevi-font` repository at the commit listed above. Six static TTF files covering weights ExtraLight through Bold were copied from `fonts/YaldeviColombo/ttf/` to the Google Fonts distribution.

**Model**: Claude Opus 4.6
