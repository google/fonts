## Summary

Yrsa was designed by Rosetta (Anna Giedryś and David Březina) as a variable serif typeface supporting Latin, Latin Extended, and Vietnamese subsets. The font was sourced from the `rosettatype/yrsa-rasa` repository at the commit listed above. Two variable font files were copied to the Google Fonts distribution: `fonts/YrsaVariable/YrsaVF-Ups.ttf` (upright) and `fonts/YrsaVariable/YrsaVF-Its.ttf` (italic), both covering weights 300 (Light) through 700 (Bold).

**Model**: Claude Opus 4.6
