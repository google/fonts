## Summary

Ysabeau was designed by Christian Thalmann as a variable sans-serif typeface supporting Latin, Cyrillic, Greek, Vietnamese, Math, and Symbols subsets. The font was sourced from the `CatharsisFonts/Ysabeau` repository at the commit listed above (shared with Ysabeau Infant, Ysabeau Office, and Ysabeau SC). Two variable font files were copied from `fonts/googlefonts/variable/` to the Google Fonts distribution: the upright and italic variants. The weight axis spans from 1 to 1000.

**Model**: Claude Opus 4.6
