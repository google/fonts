## Summary

Ysabeau SC was designed by Christian Thalmann as a variable sans-serif small caps typeface, supporting Latin, Cyrillic, Greek, Vietnamese, Math, and Symbols subsets. The font was sourced from the `CatharsisFonts/Ysabeau` repository at the commit listed above (shared with Ysabeau, Ysabeau Infant, and Ysabeau Office). One variable font file was copied from `fonts/googlefonts/variable/` to the Google Fonts distribution: `YsabeauSC[wght].ttf`. The weight axis spans from 1 to 1000.

**Model**: Claude Opus 4.6
