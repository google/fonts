## Summary

Zen Loop was designed by Yoshimichi Ohira as a static display handwriting typeface supporting Latin and Latin Extended subsets. The font was sourced from the `googlefonts/zen-loop` repository at the commit listed above. Two static TTF files were copied from `fonts/ttf/` to the Google Fonts distribution: `ZenLoop-Regular.ttf` and `ZenLoop-Italic.ttf`.

**Model**: Claude Opus 4.6
