Zephirum is designed for good readability and legibility. Its distinctive, machine-readable design makes it ideal for technical documentation, coding environments, and any application requiring clear, consistent character spacing.

The Arabic word Ṣifr (صفر), meaning "empty" or "nothing," was introduced to Europe through Islamic mathematics. Fibonacci later Latinized it as "Zephirum", which evolved into the modern word "zero."
