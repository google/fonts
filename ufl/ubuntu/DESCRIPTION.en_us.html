Ubuntu is a pleasant-to-look-at sans serif typeface with crisp readable lines, suitable for clarity on desktop and mobile computing screens. It was designed in tune with Ubuntu's philosophy which states that every user should be able to use their software in the language of their choice.

Both the final font Truetype/OpenType files and the design files used to produce the font family are distributed under an open licence and you are expressly encouraged to experiment, modify, share and improve.
